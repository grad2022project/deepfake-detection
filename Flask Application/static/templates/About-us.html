About Us

Deepfake technology is making it harder to tell whether some news you see and hear on the internet is real or not.

because it is becoming increasingly common, and the enhanced methods of creating deepfakes are making it more diffecult to detect it with human eye, we decided to contribute in the Deepfake Detection Challenge and provide an open source model that can detect deepfakes whether it was video or image. we provide the model as an online tool for the public to make it more easy to verify images and videos circulating on social media.
